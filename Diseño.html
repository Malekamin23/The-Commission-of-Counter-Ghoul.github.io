The Commission of Counter Ghoul [Ro-Ghoul]

Advanced Operational Overview

Introduction

Background

The following is a set of rules and guidelines for the Ro-Ghoul faction system. All factions are expected to follow these guidelines, not just official ones. Keep in mind that this document is prone to change over-time and update information will be posted in the Discord. We urge all official faction owners to place this document (the link) in a separate channel on their Discord to ensure that their members who are not in the Ro-Ghoul Discord may read it. Violating these rules will result in different degrees of punishment depending on the offense.

[Note: The term ‘HR’ ranges from staff, leaders, and anyone with power. ‘Faction’ refers to groups/clans]

Read if you are serious about participating in factions or leading one, do not skim.

Section 1 - Official Status and Becoming Official

Key Directives

1. Neutralize threats with surgical precision.
2. Employ cutting-edge kagune suppressors and quinque technology.
3. Ensure civilian safety while maintaining operational secrecy.

Technological Arsenal

Overview

CCG agents utilize a range of specialized equipment, including:
- Quinque: Transformative weapons crafted from ghoul kagune.
- RC Suppressors: Devices to neutralize ghoul regeneration.
- Tactical Drones: Advanced AI-assisted recon tools.

Squad Protocols

Operational Hierarchy

Squads operate under the strict command of senior investigators. Compliance with CCG protocol is mandatory to maintain order and maximize operational success.
